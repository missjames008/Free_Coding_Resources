About ETA: NOW
 

ETA:NOW is a project that connects refugees and other under-represented populations (e.g. women, people of color) with free resources that teach skills for web development and design. We believe in empowering individuals by providing them with access to the tools they need to learn a valuable, marketable new skill.

If you are an organisation offering free or discounted technology, web development, or design courses to under-represented populations and you would like to be featured on this site, please get in touch!
