About ETA: NOW

ETA:NOW is a project that began with the belief that technology is for everyone. The aim of this site is to connect populations that are traditionally under-represented in the tech sector with resources that teach skills for web development and design.
 
We believe in empowering individuals by providing them with access to the tools they need to learn a valuable, marketable new skill. In order to facilitate this, all resources linked to on the ETA:NOW site are either free or offer significant discounts (more than 50%) to under-represented populations.
 
If you are an organisation offering free or discounted technology courses and you would like to be featured on this site, please get in touch!
